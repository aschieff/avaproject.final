    Paw

    Stroop

    Stroop is a good boy. His chill additude towards life gets him all the attention from the ladies. Stroop gets his name from the famous Stroopwafel- his owners favorite snack. Speaking of snacks Stroop will do anything for a treat. His favorite trick is raising his paw.

    Chip

    Chip got his name when he was just 6 weeks old after he ate some off the ground. It's his favoirte human food! Chip lives amoung the hippies in Portland where he vibes all day long. Chip's owners love bringing him to the beach where he can chase all the seagulls.
